In 'Goncharov' acclaimed director Martin Scorsese teams up with legendary actors Robert De Niro, Al Pacino, Gene Hackman, Cybill Shepherd, and Harvey Keitel to create the most memorable mafia film ever made.

After completing a hit in Russia, Goncharov travels to Naples to lay low, but his past catches up with him when a former associate appears with a job. Meanwhile, his wife Katya is having an affair, and his former ally Andrey returns, stirring up old feelings. As Goncharov navigates the deadly world of the Neapolitan mafia, he must also confront his personal demons and settle his loyalties.
